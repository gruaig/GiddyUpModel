AT T-60 FOR EACH BET:
1. Open Betfair
2. Find the race
3. Check horse's current odds
4. Fill spreadsheet: 't60_actual_odds' column
5. If odds >= min_odds_needed → BET ✅
6. If odds < min_odds_needed → SKIP ❌

EVENING:
1. Check results
2. Fill 'result' column (WON/LOST)
3. Calculate P&L:
   WON: stake × (odds - 1) × 0.98
   LOST: -stake
   SKIP: blank
